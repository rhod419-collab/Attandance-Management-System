QUICK TIPS:

- All passwords follow pattern: [role]123
  Admin: admin123
  Teachers: teacher123
  Students: student123

- Data is stored in browser localStorage (not a database)

- Use admin account to add/edit/delete data

- Teachers can take attendance and view reports

- Students can check their records

- To reset: localStorage.clear(); location.reload();
